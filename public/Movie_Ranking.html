The Best Bollywood Movies of All Times

Top movies rated by Shalaka!

In order of my preference,

Yeh Jawani Hai Deewani(2013)

"Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani" is a delightful blend of romance, comedy, and life lessons that resonates deeply with anyone who's ever had dreams, fallen in love, or cherished friendships. Directed by Ayan Mukerji, the film beautifully captures the essence of youthful exuberance and the complexities of personal growth. It is fresh and alive and very related to splendours youth of today. This is the movie which invigorate you through entire of it's runtime. The main story of this movie is about four friends of different set who go for a adventure trip and get to know little personal aspects of each other. After the long time they reunite and equation change according the time.This was not really a romantic comedy, despite the cast lineup. It more like a smart, meaningful story.There are a lot of humorous moments but also a lot of good advice. The theme was not the only great aspect of Yeh Jawaani was the beautiful locations picked for the movie.The cinematography really brought out magic of travel and experiencing new things.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara(2011)

Zoya Akhtar's Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara, through the lives of three friends, presents the world of contemporary Indian youth, who are successful, affluent yuppies. They just enjoy life because financially they have the means to do it. In mood and execution the film is very modern, light, yet it entails complex relationships in family and between friends.

It is captivating, interesting and entertaining, and it captures the beauty of Spain with great mastery. At times this presentation is too perfect, but then it's a movie so that's what audiences want to see. It is beautifully shot, with excellent cinematography working in its favour. The background score is very good, and the songs are very fun and catchy. All these aspects create a lyrical tone that is quite uplifting to watch. Some scenes are just amazingly captured. The one when the guys get out of the water after diving, is pure magic. So is the one which shows them skydiving. It takes the light-hearted tone of a fun, all-boys road trip through Spain to give you a deep and heartfelt message on why we should live life by seizing the moment and following our hearts.

Jab We Met(2007)

Imtiaz Ali's 'Jab We Met' is cinema at its best! One of the finest Romanctic love stories to come out of Indian Cinema. A simple story told in the most interesting and enjoyable manner. The film's first hour is enjoyable, the second is exactly the same. 'Jab We Met' holds your attention from almost start to end, leaving the viewer completely satisfied. The film was a success at the box office, and has remained to be a fan favorite. Thumbs up! The sound-track is a brownie point! Although being released in the year 2007, all the songs in this movie are still popular, compelling viewers to play it again and again!
On the whole, 'Jab We Met' is a pure example of entertaining cinema. As said before, its one the finest romantic films to come out of Indian Cinema in years.
